## Overview

GPT-NeoX provides tooling and recipes for training large open-source language models, with training scripts, evaluation tools and model management utilities.

## Key features

- End-to-end training and evaluation workflows.
- Good interoperability with Hugging Face tokenizers and transformers.

## Use cases

- Research and reproducible open-source LLM training.

## Technical highlights

- PyTorch-based training with containerized examples and optimization guidance.
