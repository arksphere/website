## Introduction

Jaaz is the world's first open-source multimodal creative assistant, privacy-first and locally deployable, integrating image/video generation, infinite canvas, smart AI agent, and magic canvas for visual creation and collaboration.

## Key Features

- One-prompt image & video generation, supports multiple models (GPT-4o, Midjourney, Kling, etc.)
- Magic Canvas & Video: prompt-free creation, Lego-like combination
- Infinite canvas & visual storyboarding, real-time collaboration
- Smart AI Agent: chat to insert objects, transfer styles, control logic; supports local & cloud models
- Flexible deployment: fully offline or hybrid, built-in media & prompt library
- Privacy & security: local-first, no tracking, safe for commercial use

## Use Cases

- Visual content creation and editing
- Video dubbing and scene planning
- Multi-user collaboration and creative design
- Local secure office and business applications

## Technical Highlights

Jaaz leverages multimodal AI, supports local/cloud model orchestration, cross-platform (Windows/macOS), built-in media and prompt library, privacy-first architecture, suitable for diverse creative scenarios.
